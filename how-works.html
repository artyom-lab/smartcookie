How it works

Take SmartCookieWifi out of the box and connect it directly to your home router

On your children's device, select the WiFi settings and choose the SmartCookieWifi network

compatible with:
available on:

At your first connection, a window of our interface will pop out automatically

Set your preferences on our «Parent» interface, it gives you full control of SmartCookieWifi such as the level of questions...

Your child must simply answer the questions in order to earn cookies.

Cookies represent Internet minutes. Your children must earn enough to regain their Internet connection

The connection cycle is very simple

And win the Internet returns
